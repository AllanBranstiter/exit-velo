Data sources: Early 2026 Statcast data via FanGraphs. Baseline comparisons from 2025 full-season FanGraphs batting and pitching files. Daily projections from Razzball. All stats through April 1, 2026 (6 games).

xwOBA: Expected Weighted On-Base Average — measures the quality of contact (exit velocity + launch angle) regardless of what actually happened. League avg .315. Higher is better.
Exit Velocity: How hard the ball comes off the bat, in mph. Avg ~88 mph.
Hard-Hit Rate: Percentage of batted balls hit 95+ mph.
K%-BB%: Strikeout rate minus walk rate for pitchers. Higher = better command and swing-and-miss combination.
Expected ERA (xERA): What a pitcher's ERA should look like based on the quality of contact allowed, stripping out sequencing luck.
Chase Rate (O-Swing%): How often a batter swings at pitches outside the strike zone. Higher = more chasing = bigger concern.
