Data sources: 2025–2026 Statcast via FanGraphs. Pitcher grades from PitchingPlus/Stuff+ leaderboards. Series projections from Razzball (scaled to 4-game series). Stats through April 5, 2026.

Contact Score (xwOBA): Measures how hard and well the ball was hit, regardless of what actually happened. Higher is better for hitters, lower is better for pitchers. League avg ~.315.
Avg Exit Velocity: How hard the ball comes off the bat, in mph. Higher = harder contact.
Hard-Hit Rate: % of batted balls hit 95+ mph.
Barrel Rate: % of batted balls hit with ideal speed and angle for extra bases.
Chase Rate: % of pitches outside the zone the batter swings at. Lower is better for hitters.
Expected ERA: ERA estimate based on contact quality allowed, not results. More predictive than ERA alone.
K%-BB%: Strikeout rate minus walk rate. The best single-number summary of a pitcher's command.
